Who We Are

Built on the Land

The Rajgarhia Group is a unified family of three specialized manufacturing units, each rooted in the fertile plains of Bahraich, Uttar Pradesh. We are custodians of quality, champions of sustainable practices, and proud contributors to India's food security.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that premium products are born from respect — for the quality of our raw materials, for the land that sustains us, and for every customer who places their trust in us.

Mission &
Vision

VISION

To be a trusted name in grain processing across India — recognised for quality, innovation, and our commitment to tradition and technology working together to nourish the nation.

MISSION

To deliver premium quality grains through modern manufacturing processes, uphold rigorous quality standards, and serve our customers with integrity and long-term reliability.

Our Journey

1982
Foundation

Rajgarhia Group founded with a single rice mill in Bahraich — a humble beginning driven by quality.

1995
Wheat Expansion

Lalitom Food Products established, entering wheat flour processing with modern milling technology.

2005
Red Lentil Division

Rajgahia Food Products launched, completing our food grain trifecta and strengthening B2B presence.

2015
ISO Certification

ISO 9001:2015 achieved — formal recognition of our commitment to quality across all units.

2024
Modern Transformation

Advanced color sorting, automated packing lines, and digital traceability — heritage meets the future.

Quality Control Workflow

Our comprehensive quality assurance process ensures every product meets the highest standards — from sourcing to final packaging.

01

Raw Material Sourcing

Careful selection of premium raw materials from trusted suppliers with stringent quality checks at procurement.

02

Laboratory Testing

Advanced testing for moisture content, purity, contamination, and nutritional parameters in our in-house labs.

03

Process Monitoring

Continuous quality surveillance during processing with automated systems and manual inspections at critical points.

04

Final Product Inspection

Comprehensive testing of finished products for quality, consistency, and compliance before packaging approval.

05

Packaging Quality

Food-grade packaging materials with proper sealing, labeling, and batch coding for full traceability.

06

Documentation & Compliance

Complete record-keeping and adherence to national food safety standards and regulatory requirements.
